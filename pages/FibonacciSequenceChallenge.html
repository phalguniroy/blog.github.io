Fibonacci Sequence Challenge :

The most effective way of making habit and working towards goals.

Why there is need of FibonacciSequenceChallenge when there are already so many challenges already?

To learn new things or mastery a subject we need to put effort daily and after some period of time, we will get the result. So consistency is the key to mastery or getting good at something very well.

So, how to devlop consistency?

Before that let us understand why we are addicted towards social media, whatsap, youtube and other platforms.

The answer is instant gratification. When we use these kinds of app, We immediately get the reward as a feel-good chemical known as dopamine released in our brain.

When we get likes on Facebook or watch comedy video on youtube we feel good and as a rewarding brain gets the dopamine. So it connects the activity with pleasure and craves for it to feel good. As a result the activity turn into a habit. We classify it as bad as it is not productive for us.

activity + instant dopamine = habit

On the other hand learning or studying a subject for long time does not give instant reward but give us necessary things which are really good for us. For example: eating healthy and exercise daily will give us good health, journalling help us to Keep our thoughts organized and monitor our progress, etc.

What is FibonacciSequenceChallenge ?
